## High Contrast Colors to Help with Color Blindness
- Dark Blue (#000080)
- Light Blue (#ADD8E6)
- Dark Green (#006400)
- Light Green (#90EE90)
- Dark Red (#8B0000)
- Light Red (#FFC0CB)
- Dark Purple (#800080)
- Light Purple (#BA55D3)
- Dark Yellow (#FFD700)
- Light Yellow (#FFFFE0)
- Dark Orange (#FF8C00)
- Light Orange (#FFA500)
- Dark Brown (#8B4513)
- Light Brown (#D2691E)
- Dark Gray (#A9A9A9)
- Light Gray (#D3D3D3)
